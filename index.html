About Me

My name is Ed Mikael Bebic, and I am a 22-year-old graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering from the University of Batangas Lipa Campus. Residing in Lipa City, Batangas, I am a dedicated and fast learner with a strong work ethic and a passion for technology and innovation.

During my academic journey, I completed a 600-hour On-the-Job Training (OJT) as an IT Assistant at the Department of Public Works and Highways District Engineering Office in Lipa. This experience allowed me to develop hands-on skills in troubleshooting, hardware maintenance, and inventory management, including tracking and organizing computer-related components using Microsoft Excel.

I possess a solid foundation in programming, with proficiency in Python, C#, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Android Kotlin. I am actively working to enhance these skills through self-directed learning and practical projects. My recent endeavors include developing an object detection system using Python with YOLO and OpenCV, creating web-based interfaces with HTML and CSS, and exploring Android app development using Kotlin. Additionally, I use C# for game development in Unity, which allows me to combine technical knowledge with creative problem-solving.

I am seeking an opportunity to join an organization where I can contribute my technical skills while continuing to grow and improve as a professional. I am eager to learn from experienced mentors, tackle challenging projects, and develop innovative solutions that create value for both the company and its clients.
